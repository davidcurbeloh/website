5 November 2025
🎓 PhD milestone

PhD thesis defended! 🎉

On 5 November 2025, I defended my PhD thesis in Oceanography and Global Change at the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria. It was a very special day and the culmination of several years of work on marine carbonate-system dynamics, air–sea CO₂ exchange and ocean acidification across the North Atlantic and Mediterranean basins.

The thesis, entitled “The marine carbonate system dynamics in fragile environments: insights from the North Atlantic and Mediterranean basins”, was presented as a compendium of five research articles and received the Cum Laude distinction. 🌊

The thesis is available in open access here: https://accedacris.ulpgc.es/handle/10553/151467.

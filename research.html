Fieldwork experience

~2 months total

Oceanographic cruises

Participation in the SAGA 34.5ºS cruise from Punta Arenas to Cape Town and the SINES-EUROFLEETS+ cruise along the Portuguese coast. Work included seawater sampling, sensor deployment and onboard laboratory analyses of carbonate-system variables (AT, CT, pH) and dissolved oxygen using the Winkler method.

Skills

Computational skills

Scientific programming, data handling and reproducible workflows using MATLAB and Python. Experience with command-line interfaces, Linux data-processing workflows, supercomputing environments and LaTeX.

Technical skills

Autonomous observing systems on VOS and buoys; Sea-Bird thermometers and thermosalinographs; optodes; Pro-Oceanus dissolved gas sensors; pH meters; fluorometers; General Oceanics systems; Li-COR infrared gas analysers; multiparameter sensors.

Laboratory skills

Total alkalinity and total inorganic carbon using VINDTA instruments; dissolved oxygen using the Winkler method; pH using potentiometric and spectrophotometric methods; sediment carbon analyses including loss-on-ignition and Walkley–Black methods.

Data integration and modelling

Integration of observational datasets, satellite products, reanalysis datasets and model outputs, including FESOM2.7–REcoM3.1. Familiarity with CANYON-B, ESPER, SOCAT and GLODAP. Trend detection, time-series analysis, uncertainty assessment, error propagation and spatial-temporal diagnostics.
